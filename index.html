Bio

Hi! I'm Jiabao Ji, a third-year Ph.D. student at UCSB advised by Prof. Shiyu Chang. I also work closely with Dr. Yang Zhang.

My research focuses primarily on generative AI models. Recently, I am mainly focused on following areas:

  • Decoding-time algorithm for improving LLM trustworthiness.
  • Algorithm for enhanced controllability for genrative models.

Before joining UCSB, I obtained B.Eng. in Computer Science at ACM class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. During my undergrad, I intered at X-lance Lab at SJTU working with Prof. Kai Yu and Prof. Lu Chen. I also had a remote summer intern at SLS group working with Prof. Tianxing He and Prof. James Glass.

Latest update: I will join Apple AI/ML SII team at Cuptertino, CA as a research intern in Summer 2025.
